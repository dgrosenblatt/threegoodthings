"Consciously spending a few minutes each day focusing on some of the good things that happen can help us notice what goes right as well as wrong in our lives. Even on a bad day there are some good things that happen, however small." -Action For Happiness

This daily exercise has been recommended by Lifehacker, Action For Happiness, Lift, Live More Awesome, Happier Human, and Good.is. The goal of this site is to help spread the idea and give people an alternative to physically writing things down.

This is meant to be a reflection on what happened today. Once you leave or refresh this page, your information will not be saved. You won't be able to access what you've entered in the past, but you also don't have to worry about anyone discovering the intimate details of your life this way.
